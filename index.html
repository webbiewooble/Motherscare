Why This Hospital?

Because Every Woman Deserves Healthcare with Dignity, Privacy, and Respect.

Mother Care Women & Child Hospital was born from a simple belief: no woman should ever hesitate to seek medical care because she feels uncomfortable, unheard, or lacks privacy.

Many women find it difficult to openly discuss personal health concerns or undergo medical examinations. We believe healthcare is not only about treatment—it is about trust, comfort, confidentiality, and respect.

That is why we are building a women-centered hospital where, wherever practical and appropriate, women receive care from qualified female healthcare professionals in an environment designed around their privacy, dignity, and well-being.

Our Core Commitment
"Our mission is not driven by profit or recognition. It is driven by service."

We are committed to providing high-quality healthcare at affordable and transparent prices without compromising medical standards or patient care.

If you ever question our intentions, we invite you to visit us. Meet our team, see our facilities, compare our services, and judge us by our actions—not our words. We believe trust is earned through honesty, compassion, and consistent care.

Every woman who walks through our doors is someone's mother, daughter, sister, wife, or friend—and above all, she is a human being who deserves to be treated with dignity.

That is the hospital we are building. A place where healing begins with trust, privacy, compassion, and respect.

About SQA Social Welfare Trust

Building meaningful and lasting social impact across India.

SQA Social Welfare Trust is a charitable organization founded with a long-term vision of creating meaningful and lasting social impact across India.

Established on the principles of compassion, integrity, equality, and selfless service, the Trust is committed to improving the quality of life for individuals and families through initiatives in healthcare, education, skill development, employment support, and community welfare.

Our Journey Begins with Healthcare

We believe that a healthy woman is the foundation of a healthy family, and a healthy family is the foundation of a strong nation. Guided by this belief, our flagship initiative is Mother Care & Women's Hospital—a women-centered healthcare institution dedicated to providing compassionate, affordable, and high-quality medical care in an environment that prioritizes dignity, privacy, respect, and trust.

Beyond Healthcare

However, our vision extends far beyond building a hospital. We aspire to create opportunities that empower individuals, support underprivileged families, encourage education, promote skills and employment, and strengthen communities. Every project undertaken by the Trust is designed to create sustainable change and improve the lives of future generations.

At SQA Social Welfare Trust, we proudly serve every individual without discrimination based on religion, caste, community, language, gender, or economic background. We believe humanity is our greatest identity, and service to society is our highest responsibility.

Driven by Purpose

We are not driven by profit or recognition. We are driven by purpose—the purpose of building institutions that inspire trust, deliver meaningful impact, and stand as symbols of hope for generations to come. Through transparency, accountability, and unwavering dedication, we invite compassionate citizens, professionals, volunteers, and supporters to join us in building a healthier, more educated, and more empowered India—one family, one community, and one life at a time.

Our Mission

Building a healthier, stronger, and more compassionate India.

Our mission is to build a healthier, stronger, and more compassionate India by empowering women, supporting families, and creating opportunities that transform lives for generations to come.

At SQA Social Welfare Trust, we believe that every mother, daughter, sister, and child deserves access to quality healthcare, education, dignity, and hope—regardless of religion, caste, community, language, or financial background.

Our First Initiative

Our first and foremost initiative is the establishment of Mother Care & Women's Hospital, a women-centered charitable hospital envisioned to provide compassionate, affordable, and high-quality healthcare in an environment where privacy, respect, dignity, and trust are never compromised.

We believe that when a woman feels safe and comfortable, she can openly discuss her health concerns and receive the care she truly deserves. Wherever practical and appropriate, our goal is to provide women with care from qualified female healthcare professionals in a setting designed around their comfort and confidentiality.

But this hospital is only the beginning.

As our Trust grows, our vision extends far beyond healthcare. We aspire to expand into education, skill development, employment support, technology, community welfare, and other initiatives that help people become self-reliant and create brighter futures for their families.

We are not driven by profit, recognition, or personal success. We are driven by service and by the belief that empowering women strengthens families, empowering families strengthens communities, and empowering communities strengthens the nation.

We cannot build this vision alone.

It will take compassionate citizens who believe in giving back to society and investing in future generations. Together, we are not merely constructing a hospital—we are building trust, creating opportunity, restoring dignity, and laying the foundation for a healthier, more educated, and more united India.

Our Message

Every great movement begins with a single decision.

Every great movement begins with a single decision—the decision to take responsibility instead of waiting for someone else to act.

SQA Social Welfare Trust was not founded by a wealthy corporation or an institution with unlimited resources. We are ordinary people with an extraordinary purpose: to build institutions that improve lives and leave a lasting legacy for future generations. Our first step is Mother Care & Women's Hospital, but our vision extends far beyond a single hospital.

Our Dream for India

We dream of an India where every woman receives healthcare with dignity, every child has access to opportunity, every family can dream without fear, and every individual is treated with compassion and respect.

As our Trust grows, we aspire to expand into education, skill development, employment support, technology, and community development—creating opportunities that empower people to build better lives.

Service is Our Purpose

Our work will always remain free from discrimination. We welcome and serve every individual with equal respect, regardless of religion, caste, community, language, gender, ethnicity, or financial background. Humanity is our identity, and service is our purpose.

We know this journey will not be easy. We are not backed by unlimited wealth, but we are backed by unwavering determination. Whether this mission is completed with the support of thousands or through years of persistent effort, we will not stop. Challenges may slow us, but they will never define us. Our commitment is stronger than our circumstances.

However, together we can achieve far more than any one organization can achieve alone. We are not only seeking donations. We are looking for visionaries, mentors, doctors, healthcare professionals, educators, entrepreneurs, architects, engineers, legal experts, volunteers, philanthropists, and compassionate citizens who believe that meaningful change is possible. Your ideas, experience, guidance, encouragement, and support are as valuable as any financial contribution.

We are inviting you to become part of India's future.

If our vision inspires you, we invite you to walk beside us—not as donors alone, but as partners in building a better tomorrow. Ask questions. Guide us. Hold us accountable. Help us improve. Together, let us create institutions that stand for compassion instead of profit, dignity instead of discrimination, and service instead of self-interest.

We are not asking you to build our dream.

Together, let us build institutions that future generations will proudly inherit, and let our work remind the world that when ordinary people unite with honesty, courage, and compassion, extraordinary things become possible.
